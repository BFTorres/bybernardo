About

I am a software developer and multidisciplinary designer, currently working at Lohoff, supervising the design of a vast portfolio of clients, including BMW, Thyssenkrupp, Mini, and Rolls Royce.

I believe that some of the most simple design solutions can be the most effective, and exceptional design doesn’t have to be visually convoluted. My inspiration relies on the freedom of endless possibilities, and my passion for designing and developing products with a touch of simplicity and originality.

Born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, I moved to Germany in 2002, where I specialized as a Hotel Industry Expert at Accor. After ten years of professional experience in event management and sales, working for companies like Massimo Dutti and Hennes & Mauritz, I graduated from the Ironhack Tech School as a Fullstack Developer, subsequently starting my career in Tech.

As a self-taught designer, I spend time learning new skills; Currently, I am exploring the world of user experience design and blockchain from different angles.
